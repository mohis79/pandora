Thinking Steps

  4. Explain where hope fits in.

  • Write a short modern-day version of this myth. What is the “box” today?

Critical Thinking Steps

  1. Choose a clear opinion.
  2. Explain the other side of the argument.
  3. Use evidence from the story.
  4. Link your thinking to young people, voice, and the future.
